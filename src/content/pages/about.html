Our Story

BUILT FROM love.

Recess began in 2019 with a simple belief: every athlete deserves a place to play, grow, and belong.

Founder & Head Coach
The Founder

A COACH'S vision.

Recess started in Markham because we saw a gap — athletes with talent and drive, but nowhere that truly developed them as complete people.

We built Recess to be different. Not just a gym. A community. A system. A culture where every athlete, from the 6-year-old JR.NBA player to the Prep Academy senior, feels seen, pushed, and supported.

Five years in, we have 500+ athletes, 30+ college placements, and a community we are deeply proud of. But we're just getting started.
